Gulab Jamun

Ingredients: Milk Powder, All-Purpose Flour, Ghee, Sugar, Cardamom, Rose Water

Indulge in the sheer delight of Gulab Jamun - a classic Indian dessert that's adored for its irresistible sweetness and melt-in-the-mouth texture. These golden-brown dumplings, made from a blend of milk powder and flour, are fried until they attain a soft, spongy texture. Once cooked, they are gently immersed in fragrant rose-infused sugar syrup that imparts a rich flavor. This beloved dessert is often served warm, exuding an aroma that's as inviting as its taste.

Chole

Ingredients: Chickpeas, Onion, Tomato, Ginger, Garlic, Spices, Cilantro

Savor the rich and aromatic flavors of Chole, a beloved North Indian dish that's a staple in households and a favorite street food. Made from tender chickpeas cooked with a medley of spices, onion, and tomato, Chole is a hearty and wholesome curry that's both satisfying and flavorful. The harmonious blend of spices, including cumin, coriander, and garam masala, infuses the dish with its distinctive taste and tantalizing aroma.

Aloo Gobi

Ingredients: Cauliflower, Potatoes, Turmeric, Cumin, Garam masala, Tomato, Green peas, Oil, Salt

Aloo Gobi is a delightful North Indian dish that combines tender cauliflower florets and potatoes with a medley of aromatic spices. Turmeric, cumin, and coriander infuse this dish with a rich, earthy flavor, while garam masala adds warmth and depth. Tomatoes and green peas provide a burst of color and freshness. Cooked to perfection in oil and seasoned with a touch of salt, Aloo Gobi is a comforting and satisfying vegetarian classic that's sure to please the palate.
